Entry Management Application

This is a simple Entry Management Application which you can use to keep track of the entries of the guest in your office.

If you are a host, then you can register yourself. After that you can login into your account to see the guests that have checked in and out. Email and message notification is also triggered when a guest checks in, therefore please enter valid email and phone number.

If you are a Guest, then you can checkin into any registered host office by filling a simple form. When you will checkout, a message and mail will be sent to you with the details of your visit, therefore please enter a valid email and phone number.
